About Heartbeat Interactive

Founded in 2024, Heartbeat Interactive is an independent game development studio committed to reshaping the landscape of interactive entertainment. We specialise in bold, story-driven experiences that challenge conventions and spark imagination.

Our team of passionate developers, artists and designers collaborate to craft games that connnect with players on deep, emotional levels. We believe in the power of innovation, the impact of storytelling and the magic of gameplay that leaves a lasting imperession.
