About Heartbeat Interactive

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate game developers, Heartbeat Interactive started as a small collective working out of a garage. Today, we're a team of 15 developers, artists, and storytellers dedicated to creating games that make players feel something special.

We believe games are more than entertainment—they're experiences that can touch hearts, challenge minds, and create lasting memories.

Our Mission

"To create games with emotional depth, innovative mechanics, and artistic vision that leave a lasting impact on players."

Passion-Driven

Every project begins with passion. We only work on games we truly believe in and would love to play ourselves.

Innovative Design

We push boundaries with unique mechanics and storytelling approaches that challenge conventional game design.

Player-Focused

Our development process always keeps the player experience at the forefront of every decision we make.
